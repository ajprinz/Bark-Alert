Leaving pets locked in cars is never safe. But when the weather gets warmer, it can be deadly. High temperatures can cause irreparable organ damage and even death. Protecting animals from an unnecessary death is a problem we can all agree to prevent.

Facts

  1. When it's 72 degrees Fahrenheit outside, the temperature inside your car can heat up to 116 degrees Fahrenheit within an hour.
  2. When it's 80 degrees Fahrenheit outside, the temperature inside your car can heat up to 99 degrees Fahrenheit within 10 minutes.
  3. Rolling down the windows has been shown to have little effect on the temperature inside a car.

Mission Statement

Our mission as the Bark Alert team is to ensure the safety of dogs all around the world. We want to get our product attached to every dog collar, so no dog will ever be harmed by warm temperatures of cars.

Our Solution

To solve the problem, we have a collar attachment that sends an alert to the app in real time when a harmful temperature is reached.

To go along with our collar attachment, we created an app to monitor your dog's temperature, as well as provide emergency numbers and locations of vets nearest to you.
